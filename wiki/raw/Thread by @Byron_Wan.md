**Byron Wan** @Byron\_Wan [2026-04-03](https://x.com/Byron_Wan/status/2039897160959861156)

Apr 1: Junjie Zhang, aka Jeff Zhang, a naturalized US citizen originally from China, pleaded guilty after lying to US customs officials while attempting to board a flight to China with proprietary information belonging to his employer — Spirit AeroSystems, an aviation company in Wichita.  
4 月 1 日：張俊傑（Junjie Zhang，又名 Jeff Zhang），一名原籍中國的美國歸化公民，在試圖攜帶其雇主——位於威奇託的航空企業 Spirit AeroSystems 的專有信息登上飛往中國的航班時，向美國海關官員撒謊，他對此表示認罪。  
  
Zhang pleaded guilty to one count of making a false statement.  
張某承認犯有一項作虛假陳述罪。  
  
Zhang’s position as a senior material and process engineer provided him access to confidential data and proprietary information. In 2018, Zhang’s employer reported him to the FBI after an incident during a work trip to China where he displayed suspicious behavior.  
張某身為高階材料與製程工程師，能夠接觸到機密資料和專有資訊。 2018年，張某在一次赴華出差期間行為可疑，其雇主隨後向聯邦調查局舉報了他。  
  
In Sep 2019, Customs and Border Protection (CBP) stopped Zhang at an airport in Dallas, Texas, as he was attempting to board a flight to China. During an interview, agents asked if he had any work-related information on his electronic devices to which he responded, “no”. Zhang told the agents the thumb drive and laptop he carried only contained personal information.  However, when CBP agents examined the devices, they discovered documents belonging to Zhang’s employer marked “Proprietary” and “Confidential” along with graphs and blueprints associated with the aviation company’s work. Zhang then changed his story to say that his employer had given him permission to have the documents.  
2019 年 9 月，美國海關與邊境保護局（CBP）在德州達拉斯機場攔下了張某，當時他正準備登機前往中國。在詢問過程中，CBP 官員詢問他電子設備中是否有任何與工作相關的信息，張某回答“沒有”。張某告訴官員，他攜帶的 USB 和筆記型電腦只包含個人資訊。然而，CBP 官員檢查這些設備後，發現了屬於張某雇主的文件，這些文件被標記為“專有”和“機密”，此外還有一些與該航空公司業務相關的圖表和藍圖。隨後，張某改口稱，他的雇主允許他持有這些文件。  
  
CBP alerted the FBI who contacted Zhang’s employer. The company informed the FBI that Zhang was not authorized to have confidential documents on his personal devices or to leave the country with that information.  
美國海關與邊境保護局（CBP）通知了聯邦調查局（FBI），FBI 隨後聯繫了張某的雇主。該公司告知 FBI，張某無權在其個人設備上儲存機密文件，也無權攜帶這些資訊離開美國。  
  
The proprietary data on Zhang’s devices is estimated to be valued at more than $100,000.  
張某設備上的專有數據估計價值超過 10 萬美元。  
  
Zhang is scheduled to be sentenced on July 23, 2026.  
張某的判決日期定於2026年7月23日。  
  
Wichita is sometimes known as the “Air Capital of the World” due to its links to well-established US aircraft manufacturers and systems suppliers. Some of the biggest aviation companies based in the city include Cessna, Textron Aviation, Learjet, and parts manufacturer Spirit AeroSystems. European aerospace giant Airbus also has a major presence in the city.  
由於與許多美國知名飛機製造商和系統供應商的緊密聯繫，威奇託有時被稱為「世界航空之都」。一些總部位於該市的大型航空企業包括塞斯納、德事隆航空、裡爾噴射機以及零件製造商斯皮里特航空系統公司。歐洲航空航太巨頭空中巴士公司在該市也擁有重要的業務。  
  
Zhang could have been attempting to supply the intellectual property to rival aerospace firms based in China, although these allegations were never pursued by prosecutors.  
張某可能試圖將智慧財產權提供給中國境內的競爭對手航空航太公司，儘管檢察官從未追究這些指控。  
  
Zhang’s attempted trip to China came at a pivotal time for the development of the Chinese-made COMAC C919 single-aisle aircraft, which is a direct competitor to the Boeing 737 and Airbus A320 series lines.  
張先生此次訪華之行正值中國商飛 C919 單通道飛機研發的關鍵時期，該機型是波音 737 和空中巴士 A320 系列飛機的直接競爭對手。
